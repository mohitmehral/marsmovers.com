Wow Facts

Things That'll Blow
Your Mind About Mars

7 months
That's how long it takes to reach Mars. You'll travel 480 million km on a curved Hohmann transfer orbit — not a straight line.
NASA Trajectory Data
4–24 min
Light delay between Earth and Mars. Send a text, wait up to 24 minutes for it to arrive. No real-time calls. Ever.
Speed of Light · Physics
-140°C
Winter night at the poles. Your blood would literally boil AND freeze simultaneously due to low pressure. Mars doesn't play.
NASA Mars Climate Data
100+ robots
Humanity has sent over 50 missions to Mars. Only about half succeeded. Mars has a reputation for destroying spacecraft.
NASA/ESA Mission Archives
Sunsets are blue
On Mars, sunsets appear blue because fine dust particles scatter red light forward and blue light toward the observer. Opposite of Earth.
NASA Curiosity Rover Imagery
No magnetic field
Mars lost its global magnetic field ~4 billion years ago. Without it, solar wind stripped the atmosphere. That's why it's so thin today.
NASA MAVEN Mission

Mission Timeline

Humanity's Road
to Mars

1965
Mariner 4 — First Mars Flyby
First close-up photos of Mars. Revealed a cratered, barren world with thin atmosphere.
NASA
1976
Viking 1 & 2 — First Landing
First spacecraft to land on Mars and send back surface photos. Conducted biology experiments searching for life.
NASA
1997
Pathfinder & Sojourner — First Rover
Sojourner became the first wheeled vehicle on another planet.
NASA/JPL
2004
Spirit & Opportunity — Water Evidence
Found definitive evidence Mars once had liquid water. Opportunity lasted 15 years instead of 90 days.
NASA/JPL
2012
Curiosity — Habitable Environment
Confirmed Mars once had conditions suitable for microbial life. Found organic molecules in ancient rocks.
NASA/JPL
2021
Perseverance & Ingenuity — First Flight
Ingenuity achieved first powered flight on another planet. Perseverance collecting samples for Earth return.
NASA/JPL
2024
ESCAPADE — Mars Space Weather
Two spacecraft studying how solar wind strips Mars' atmosphere. Critical for protecting future humans.
NASA Heliophysics
2026
SpaceX Starship — First Cargo
Planned uncrewed Starship landing. Testing systems for future human missions.
SpaceX Announced
2028
Mars Sample Return
NASA/ESA mission to bring Perseverance's rock samples back to Earth. First round-trip Mars mission.
NASA/ESA Planned
2030s
First Humans on Mars
Both NASA and SpaceX targeting crewed missions. The first human footprint on Mars could happen this decade.
NASA + SpaceX Roadmap
