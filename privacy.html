Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

1. Introduction

Mars Movers ("we," "our," or "us"), operated by Apnok, is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website www.marsmovers.com.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Email address provided voluntarily through our contact forms or "Request Access" submissions.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, browser type, device type, operating system, referring URLs, and IP address — collected automatically via Google Analytics.
  • Cookies: Small data files placed on your device by Google Analytics and Google AdSense to track usage patterns and serve relevant advertisements.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and requests for access
  • Send updates about our services, roadmap, and milestones (only if you opt in)

  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google Analytics (G-N9J8L4ZB0Q): Tracks website usage. Google Privacy Policy

  • GitHub Pages: Hosts our website. GitHub Privacy Statement

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies for analytics and advertising purposes. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect site functionality.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information (email addresses) for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, or until you request deletion. Analytics data is retained per Google's standard retention policies.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Request data portability
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

9. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child, we will delete it promptly.

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. By using our website, you consent to such transfers.
